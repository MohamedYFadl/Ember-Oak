Our Story

Passion Cooked
Over Open Flame

Ember & Oak was born from a single obsession: the transformative power of fire on food. Founded in 2012 by Chef Marco Delacroix, every dish that leaves our kitchen carries the story of that flame—the char, the smoke, the caramelized perfection only heat can create.

We partner with local farms within 60 miles to source ingredients at peak freshness, letting seasonality guide our ever-evolving menu. Our open kitchen invites you to watch the craft unfold in real time.
